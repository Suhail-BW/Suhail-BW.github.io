Data alignment problem of VLMs

Masters thesis, Nov-2024 - present

Vision-language data often suffer from misalignment—images may lack textual descriptions and vice versa. Completely filtering out high-quality data is impractical in fields like Earth observation or medical imaging.
In my master's thesis, I explore how misalignment affects VL models (CLIP, SLIP, ViLBERT) by analyzing their performance under controlled alignment scenarios.
